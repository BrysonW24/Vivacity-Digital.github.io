Vivacity Support and Maintenance
Monthly Plans and Details

Whether you simply need maintenance updates or you need someone on call to answer your website how-to questions, we have you covered. Our support and maintenance contracts will help keep your site secure, up to date, and provide our expert development and consulting services for you to utilize when needed.

The digital world is an open source, dynamic and constantly changing which is why the online community continues to improve and update the software at very fast rate. These functional and security updates are required to keep websites safe, secure, and functionally robust.

Why You Need Professional Support and Maintenance

 While many websites owners think they will keep up with software updates, this fails to happen. Website owners frequently get caught up with life, work, and publishing content. In doing so, they completely forget about keeping their website software updated and their data backed up.

Websites using applications are just like any software used on desktops, tablets, or phones. It needs to be regularly updated for functional and security updates. If users fail to do so, they open themselves up to broken functionality, malware, and Google warnings of security issues.

Website maintenance contracts are great for providing peace of mind and security for your website while also protecting your online marketing efforts. Vivacity Digital supports you with this as we want the best for you and your businesses' online presence, take a look at our deals.

Additional Plan Details

Security Monitoring – Hackers are trolling the web and constantly looking for websites and blogs that are vulnerable. Once found they’ll load malware and malicious code into your website and server, which can reroute traffic and produce warning notices in Google’s organic search.

We’ll monitor your website, scan for malware, and use brute force protection to better protect you.

Uptime Monitoring – We’ll actively monitor the website or blog’s uptime and take swift action should your site go down. We’ll notify you of any issues and we’ll help get it back up as soon as possible.

SEO Reports – Our premium and professional plans include monthly SEO reports delivered right to your inbox. We’ll send you details on website visits, bounce rates, sources of traffic, top SEO keywords, top content, and conversion data.

SEO Audits – Our professional plan will provide a monthly report of technical SEO issues that can hamper your organic SEO efforts. Each audit report will produce a list of issues to address. You can update this on your own or use your personalized support time to have us resolve these for you.
