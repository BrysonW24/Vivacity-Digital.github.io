What We Do...

Your corporate (or nonprofit) website is one of the most important brand assets and marketing tools that you have. It's where your audience finds you online, learns more about your company, gets timely and critical information, conducts business, researches your products and services and builds trust.

If you're going to cut corners on cost somewhere, your website should not be near the top of the list.

We take pride in the services we offer and do everything ourselves, nothing is ever secretly outsourced or developed overseas (which is all too common these days). We do work with local service providers who compliment our business to provide a total package.

WE OFFER THE FOLLOWING CURRENT SERVICES FOR YOU:

WEB-DESIGN

A website is today the most essential part of any business irrespective of its nature. Whether you are a florist or a super market or a home delivery pizza chain – your brand needs a website.

GOOGLE ANALYTICS

Directly reach the people who are important to your business. Don’t waste money connecting with people who don’t care about your product or service. Instead, focus your funds to reach your ideal audience.

FACEBOOK AD CAMPAIGNS

Facebook can automatically optimize your ad to have your “most clicked on” image appear first, thus increasing the likelihood of Facebook users visiting your site after seeing it. Along with much more..

WEB HOSTING + DOMAIN 

Vivacity Digital will help you source your website needs to get online and ready. Your online presence is important to us, so we want to make it a smooth as possible for you.
